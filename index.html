These last two years (2020-2021), Edison has been focused on learning Software development basis as: Data structures, algorithms, C programming, Python, OOP, Linux, Systems Engineering/Devops and more. Especially since the end of the last year, he has been training on Machine Learning (ML) topics like Neural networks, computer vision, Hidden Markov models, Gaussian processes.

He worked as a software developer for 2 months at Truora, for a project in the academy he is studying. There, he worked developing an anonymous forum applying technologies as Vue, Aws services, Golang and Javascript. There, he experienced the work dynamics on a real company.

Finally, Edison expects finishing his studies on ML at the end of september. He is interested on AI companies and is open to remote work, feel free to contact him and thanks for your time.
